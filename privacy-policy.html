Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Kala Point Condominium Association, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name and email address when you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter or contact us through our online forms.

We also collect non-personal information, such as browser type and IP address, to help us understand how visitors interact with our site and to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you community updates and newsletters you have subscribed to.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • To improve our website's functionality and user experience.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our online services.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
